History of SH University


SH University was established in 1995 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia with the aim of providing high-quality education to students from all over the country and abroad. Originally a small institution with just a few faculties, it has grown over the decades into a leading university known for academic excellence, research, and community engagement.


The university offers a wide range of programs across multiple faculties, including Engineering, Business, Science, Arts, and Law. Throughout its history, SH University has focused on fostering innovation, critical thinking, and leadership skills among its students.


Over the years, SH University has built strong partnerships with international institutions, enabling student exchange programs, collaborative research, and global exposure. Its commitment to holistic education ensures that graduates are well-prepared for professional careers and societal contributions.


Today, SH University continues to uphold its tradition of excellence, integrity, and inclusivity, striving to create a vibrant learning environment for students, faculty, and the wider community.

Vision, Mission, Value & Objective


Vision: To be a leading institution in education and research, nurturing innovative and responsible global citizens.


Mission: To provide high-quality education, foster critical thinking, and support lifelong learning for all students.


Values: Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Collaboration guide everything we do.


Objective: To enhance academic excellence, promote research and innovation, develop skilled graduates, and contribute positively to society.
